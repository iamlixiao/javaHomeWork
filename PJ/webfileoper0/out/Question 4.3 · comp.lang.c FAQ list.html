Q:Does *p++ increment p,or what it points to?

A:The postfix ++ and -- operatorsessentiallyhave higher precedence than the prefix unary operators.Therefore,*p++is equivalent to *(p++);itincrements p,and returnsthe valuewhich p pointed to before p was incremented.To increment the value pointed to by p,use (*p)++(or perhaps ++*p,if theevaluationorder of theside effectdoesn't matter).
